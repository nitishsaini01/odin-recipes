Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and herbs. It is baked until the cheese melts and becomes golden.

This dish is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. It is rich, flavorful, and very satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef with garlic and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers.
  5. Bake in the oven for 30–40 minutes.
  6. Let it cool slightly and serve.

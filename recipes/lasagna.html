Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish made with sheets of pasta, meat or vegetable sauce, cheese, and creamy béchamel sauce.

It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a rich and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook onion and prepare sauce.
  3. Layer lasagna sheets in a baking dish.
  4. Add sauce and cheese layers.
  5. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  7. Bake for 30–40 minutes.
  8. Let it cool and serve.

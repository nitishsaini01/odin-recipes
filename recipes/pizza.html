Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with dough, tomato sauce, cheese and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetables or pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake in oven for 15–20 minutes.

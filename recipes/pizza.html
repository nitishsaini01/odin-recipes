Pizza

Pizza is a classic Italian dish loved worldwide, made with a crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, melted cheese, and various toppings of your choice.

Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a fun snack with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and warm water to make dough.
  2. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll the dough into a pizza base.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over the base.
  5. Add cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
Tip: Use fresh mozzarella and preheat the oven for a crispier crust.

Pizza

Description

Pizza is a famous Italian dish made with a flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

It is baked in an oven until the crust becomes crispy and the cheese melts perfectly. Pizza is loved all around the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup vegetables or toppings
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Sprinkle oregano.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  7. Slice and serve hot.

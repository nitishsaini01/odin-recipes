Samosa

Samosa is a popular Indian snack made with a crispy fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and herbs. It is usually served hot with chutney.

This savory snack is loved for its crunchy texture and delicious spicy filling. Samosas are often enjoyed during tea time or as street food across India.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare dough using flour, salt, and water.
  2. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  3. Cook onions, peas, and spices in a pan.
  4. Add mashed potatoes and mix well.
  5. Roll the dough and shape it into cones.
  6. Fill the cones with the potato mixture.
  7. Seal the edges and deep fry until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot with chutney.
Tip: Serve samosas hot with mint chutney for the best flavor.

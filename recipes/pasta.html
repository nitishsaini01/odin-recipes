Pasta

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made with wheat noodles served with a variety of sauces such as tomato, cream, or pesto.

It is quick to cook and can be customized with vegetables, cheese, and herbs for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Cook onion and garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce and vegetables.
  5. Mix the cooked pasta.
  6. Add cheese and seasoning.
  7. Serve hot.

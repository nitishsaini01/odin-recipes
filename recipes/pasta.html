Pasta

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian food made from wheat and served with delicious sauces.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Cream or tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Prepare the sauce.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce.
  4. Serve with cheese.

Pasta

Pasta is an Italian favorite, cooked to perfection and tossed in a creamy or tomato-based sauce, often with vegetables or meat.

A quick and satisfying meal for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any type)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: vegetables or chicken

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add cream and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add pasta and toss to coat.
  5. Mix in cheese and optional vegetables or chicken.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with extra parmesan if desired.
Tip: Save some pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed.
